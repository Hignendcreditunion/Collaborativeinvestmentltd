About Collaborative Investment Ltd

Driving sustainable business growth through strategic management and innovative investment solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Collaborative Investment Ltd emerged from a vision to transform Nigeria's SME landscape. What began as a young, dynamic team with big dreams has evolved into a structured investment firm managing diverse business portfolios across multiple sectors.

Our journey started with AIG Poultry Ventures and has expanded to include block manufacturing, aquaculture, and various other ventures. We believe in the power of professional management to unlock the true potential of small and medium enterprises.
